to the Playground In the divided cities of Piltover and Zaun, sisters Vi and Powder face a dangerous heist that changes their lives forever.

Vi and Powder struggle to survive in Zaun, while Jayce and Viktor make a groundbreaking discovery in Piltover.

Piltover celebrates Progress Day, but chaos erupts as Powder’s actions lead to devastating consequences.

5.Everybody Wants to Rule the World:

Vi seeks revenge, while Powder transforms into Jinx, embracing her new identity. Jayce and Viktor’s partnership faces challenges.

6.When These Walls Come Tumbling Down:

The conflict between Piltover and Zaun reaches a boiling point, and Jinx’s actions threaten to destroy everything.

7.The Boy Savior:

Jayce takes drastic measures to protect Piltover, while Vi and Caitlyn form an unlikely alliance to stop Jinx.

Oil and Water:

The fates of Piltover and Zaun hang in the balance as Jinx unleashes her final plan, forcing everyone to confront their past.

9.The Monster You Created:

In a climactic showdown, Vi and Jinx face each other, and the future of both cities is forever changed.
