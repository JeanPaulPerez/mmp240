Classic Veggie Stir-Fry

This quick and easy veggie stir-fry is a healthy, colorful dish packed with fresh vegetables and a savory sauce. It’s perfect for a light lunch or dinner and can be served with rice or noodles for a more filling meal. Ready in under 20 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or sesame oil
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 medium bell pepper (any color), sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (low-sodium preferred)
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice or noodles (optional, for serving)

Steps:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Wash and chop all vegetables. Mince the garlic and ginger.
  2. Heat Oil: In a large skillet or wok, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook Garlic and Ginger: Add the minced garlic and ginger to the skillet. Sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add Vegetables: Toss in the broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, snap peas, and zucchini. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Add Sauce: Pour in the soy sauce and hoisin sauce (if using). Stir well to coat the vegetables evenly.
  6. Serve: Transfer the stir-fry to a plate or bowl. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve with rice or noodles if desired.

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories:120
  • Protein:4g
  • Carbohydrates:15g
  • Fiber:5g
  • Sugar:7g
  • Fat:5g(mostly healthy fats from oil)
  • Sodium:500mg(varies based on soy sauce used)
